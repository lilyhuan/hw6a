Our Story

We’re a budding bakery aiming to satisfy the sweet cravings of all sorts of folks! With our handmade cinnamon rolls, brimming with flavor and love, baked fresh everyday, we live to deliver these sweets to you.

There is currently one store located in the University of Pittsburgh area. It’s a lovely store, come check it out! Alternatively, we deliver to anyone in the greater Pittsburgh area.
